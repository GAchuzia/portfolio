# Write Like Nobody's Reading

2026-03-11

Chances are, no one really is. As difficult as it is to expose myself like this on the internet, I want to become a better writer. I can't think of a better way to improve my writing skills than by starting a blog.

The idea here is that with practice, consistency, and time, I'll become am okay writer (if not a good one). I also feel like writing is the most deliberate form of thinking, and who doesn't like being good at that?

Anyway, I can't wait for someone I know to read this and then tell me about it so I can delete this whole blog and pretend it was never real.
